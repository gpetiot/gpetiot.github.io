Research

Interests:

  • software verification
  • combined static and dynamic analysis
  • runtime verification
  • functional programming
Currently working on fault injection and software countermeasure evaluation with Marie-Laure Potet and Louis Dureuil at VERIMAG. Previously worked on combination of deductive verification and automatic test generation in Frama-C with Nikolai Kosmatov, Jacques Julliand and Alain Giorgetti at CEA LIST and Univ. de Franche-Comté. Also worked on runtime verification of E-ACSL annotations with Nikolai Kosmatov and Julien Signoles at CEA LIST.
