Guillaume Petiot

Postdoctoral researcher at VERIMAG

Research

Interests:

  • software verification
  • combined static and dynamic analysis
  • functional programming
Currently working on fault injection and software countermeasure evaluation with Marie-Laure Potet and Louis Dureuil at VERIMAG. Previously worked on combination of deductive verification and automatic test generation with Nikolai Kosmatov, Jacques Julliand and Alain Giorgetti at CEA LIST and Univ. de Franche-Comté. Also worked on runtime verification of E-ACSL annotations with Nikolai Kosmatov and Julien Signoles at CEA LIST.
