Guillaume Petiot

guillaume@tarides.com

I am a software engineer at Tarides where I am involved in developing the OCaml tooling and in open-sourcing the projects of Tarides' customers. My open-source work is available on github.

Previously, I conducted a Ph.D in computer science at the Software Safety Laboratory of CEA LIST, about combining static and dynamic analyses for formal software verification. I joined the VERIMAG laboratory as a postdoctoral researcher where I studied software protections and countermeasures to counter fault injections in software. I then joined Systerel to work on the software development of the carborne controller of a CBTC system, and worked on improving the test methodology of the embedded software.
